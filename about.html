Introduction about UK

The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, commonly known as the United Kingdom or Great Britain, or simply UK is a sovereign state in Western Europe. It consists of 4 countries, England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland, and a few small islands. The UK is an island country along with Republic of Ireland, and it shares its land borderds with the same. The United Kingdom is surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean, with the North Sea to its east, the English Channel to its south and the Celtic Sea to its south-south-west. United Kingdom is the 78th-largest sovereign state in the world and the 11th-largest in Europe. It is also the 21st-most populous country, with an estimated 65.1 million inhabitants.

The United Kingdom is a constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary democracy. The monarch is Queen Elizabeth II, who has reigned since 6 February 1952, and the Prime Minister is Theresa May, assumed office on 11 July 2016.
